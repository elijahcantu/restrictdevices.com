How to Use Plucky Lockbox

  • Go to lockbox.pluckeye.net.
    • Click "Create a New Account."
    • Enter an email.
    • Note an account on lockbox.pluckeye.net is not connected to u.pluckeye.net and vice versa.
    • Check your email and verify your account.
  • Login and click "new box."
    • Enter box name (optional).
    • Enter information to store (could be passwords for accounts you can use to bypass restrictions, etc.)
    • Enter unlock delay (how long to open the box).
    • Enter the relock delay (how long for the box to close on its own, you can manually once it is open).
    • Enter the self-destruct date (how long before the box deletes itself, up to 5 years, you can change this date anytime).
    • Click "Create it!"
    • Click on a lockbox, you can edit it, unlock, relock, or delete it.
