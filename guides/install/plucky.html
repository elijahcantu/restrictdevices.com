Plucky (Pluckeye) Installation Guide

Visit getplucky.net to download the Windows version.

  • Go to getplucky.net for macOS.
    • Click on "Installer".
    • Open "Plucky.pkg".
    • Click "Continue" and use your Touch ID or password to complete the installation.
    • Click "Allow" on the popup "'Plucky' Would Like to Filter Network Content".
    • "Single Eye Software LLC" will be added to "Allow in the Background" in "Login Items" Settings.
    • Click "Open System Settings" on the popup "System Extension Blocked".
      • Click "Allow" in "Security Settings" for "System software from application 'Plucky' was blocked from loading" and use your Touch ID or password.
    • Enable "Full Disk Access" for Plucky in "Privacy & Security" System Settings.
  • chrome-extension://.../ui/congratulations.html should have opened and said "Congratulations! You have successfully installed Plucky!"
    • If you are still on catch.pluckeye.net/congratulations and it says "Installed? Plucky is not detected." Restart your browser.
